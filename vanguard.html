Frontline Assault Division

Vanguard Company serves as the primary ground combat arm of Nemesis Armed Forces, specializing in decisive engagement and territorial control.

Main Battalions

Spartan Battalion

Offensive assault operations and breach tactics. Designed for aggressive forward momentum and shock engagement.

Garrison Battalion

Defensive control, fortification, and area denial. Maintains captured territory and protects strategic assets.

Titan Battalion

Armored warfare and heavy vehicle deployment. Provides breakthrough capability and mobile cover support.

Operational Doctrine

Vanguard units operate on coordinated pressure tactics: Spartan initiates engagement, Titan creates breach superiority, and Garrison secures and stabilizes the operational zone.
